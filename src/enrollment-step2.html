Academic Requirements Checklist

Senior High School (Grades 11-12)

✓ New Students/Freshmen:
  • • Junior High School Report Card (Grade 10)
  • • Junior High School Diploma/Certificate of Completion
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • • 2x2 ID Picture (2 copies)
  • • Certificate of Enrollment (if transferring mid-year)
✓ Transferees:
  • • Form 137 (Permanent Record) - forwarded directly from previous school
  • • Latest Report Card from previous school
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • • Certificate of Transfer Credential
  • • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • • 2x2 ID Picture (2 copies)
  • • Academic Transcript from previous Senior High School (if applicable)

College Enrollment

New Students/Freshmen

  • • Senior High School Report Card (Grade 12)
  • • Senior High School Diploma/Certificate of Completion
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • • 2x2 ID Picture (3 copies)
  • • Certificate of Enrollment (if transferring mid-year)
  • • College Entrance Exam Results (if required by institution)
  • • Certificate of Eligibility for College (if applicable)

Transferees

  • • Form 137 (Permanent Record) - forwarded directly from previous school
  • • Official Transcript of Records (TOR) - forwarded directly from previous school
  • • Certificate of Transfer Credential
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • • 2x2 ID Picture (3 copies)
  • • Certificate of Enrollment (if transferring mid-year)
  • • College Entrance Exam Results (if required by institution)
  • • Certificate of Eligibility for College (if applicable)
  • • Academic Evaluation from previous college (if applicable)

Important Notes:

  • • Form 137 and TOR: These documents are forwarded directly from the previous school to the new school
  • • Public vs Private Schools: Requirements are generally the same for both types of institutions
  • • Additional Requirements: Some schools may require additional documents based on their specific policies
  • • Document Authentication: All documents must be original or certified true copies
  • • Deadlines: Submit all requirements before the enrollment deadline to avoid delays
